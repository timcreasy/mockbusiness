Our Mission

Orange Waterbottles is committed to finding alternative resources to provide humanity with a cleaner, streamlined waterbottle. Our commitment to you is unwavering, we strive to deliver you water in style.

Company History

After spending weeks lost alone in the desert, Tim Creasy decided enough was enough. With lips parched and severe dehydration, he decided he would revolutionize the water delivery market. For too long, when water bottles run empty, the require a water source to refill. Tim found himself in the desert, hallucinating, and an idea came to him: what if the water bottle could fill itself? This is the moment of conception for Orange Waterbottles in 1998. Multiple product iterations later, we present to you, Orange Waterbottles. Defying the laws of the physical world since 1998.
